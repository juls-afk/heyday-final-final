Where it all started......


Initially Brewed in 2023--Heyday Café was founded in the vibrant university belt of Manila with a simple mission: to create a welcoming space where people could enjoy great coffee and connect.

Inspired by the idea that every day can be a "heyday," the café quickly became a favorite spot for students and locals, offering a blend of quality coffee and a cozy atmosphere. From its humble beginnings, Heyday has grown into a beloved community hub.

A café culture where coffee is more than a drink

At Heyday Café, we believe that coffee is more than just a beverage—it’s an experience that brings people together, sparks creativity, and fuels dreams.
